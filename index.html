ABOUT THE COMPANY

Our team specializes in metal fabrication, die-cutting, and metalworking to deliver high-quality, tailored metalwork solutions. As a single-source provider, we’ve been tackling difficult-to-solve metalworking challenges with cutting-edge techniques since 1988. Prompt aims at creating, enhanced and sustainable value in its business areas of Turbo machinery and Power Engineering delivering end to end solutions, right from engineering to delivering power to the end user. The guiding principles create a framework for developing breakthrough technology in its continual product invention, towards pursuit of safety, reliability, value for money, innovation and sustainability.
